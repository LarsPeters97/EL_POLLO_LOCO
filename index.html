Game Explanation

Move to the right with the character Pepe and try to collect all the coins and bottles. Pepe needs the bottles at the end to throw the final boss several times and finally kill him. Pepe can throw a bottle every 500 milleseconds. But watch out, the final boss moves towards Pepe and gets faster and faster after each bottle hit. Pepe can kill the normal and small chickens by jumping on them from above. When Pepe runs against the chickens, he loses health points quickly. Good luck !!!
